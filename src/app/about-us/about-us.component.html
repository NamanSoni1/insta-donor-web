About InstaDonor

InstaDonor is a blood donation mobile application designed to connect blood donors with individuals or organizations in need of blood. The app allows users to register as donors, find nearby blood donation centers, schedule appointments to donate blood, and receive notifications about urgent blood donation needs or upcoming local donation events.

Additionally, it provide information about eligibility criteria for donors, health benefits of donating blood, and guidelines for safe and successful donations. The app aims to facilitate the process of donating and acquiring blood, ultimately contributing to saving lives in times of medical emergencies.
