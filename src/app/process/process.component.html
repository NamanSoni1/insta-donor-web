Donation Process

The donation process from the time you arrive center until the time you leave

1 - Registration

Donors are required to fill out a registration form that includes personal information such as name, contact details, address, date of birth, and other relevant details like about the donor's health history and lifestyle.

2 - Health History

A healthcare professional may conduct an interview with the donor to discuss their health history, recent travel, medications, and other factors that could impact the safety of the donation

3 - Donation

Once cleared, the donor is directed to the donation area. A trained phlebotomist collects a unit of blood using sterile techniques. The actual blood donation process typically takes around 10-15 minutes.

4 - Save Life

Giving blood is a uniquely powerful act. Effective coordination can dramatically increase its life-saving impact
